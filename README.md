## Project Overview

Wholesale vegetable and fish prices from Kalimati Market — one of Nepal's largest produce markets — were analyzed using Microsoft Excel. The analysis focused on identifying pricing patterns, seasonal trends, and price stability across different items.

---

## Key Findings

Based on the analysis of price data across 2021, 2022, and 2023:

| Finding | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Price Peak** | Q3-Q4 of 2023 reached **रु 152 per kg** |
| **Most Stable Item** | Tomato prices remained consistent at **रु 64-68 per kg** across all three years |
| **Seasonal Pattern** | Prices tend to rise in Q3-Q4, likely due to monsoon supply constraints |

---

## Data Source

- **Market:** Kalimati Fruit & Vegetable Market, Kathmandu, Nepal
- **Period:** 2021 – 2023
- **Items Analyzed:** Vegetables and fish (including Tomato, Rahu, and others)

## Key Takeaways

- **Tomatoes** are the most price-predictable vegetable, maintaining a narrow range (रु 64-68/kg) across three years
- **Q3-Q4 2023** saw the highest recorded prices (रु 152/kg), suggesting seasonal supply factors
- Price stability is not uniform across all items — some vegetables show significantly higher volatility
